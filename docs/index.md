# Privacy Policy for AutoScreenShot

**Last updated: July 17, 2026**

AutoScreenShot is a utility app developed by **joneschean** to help users capture long on-screen content, such as chat histories or web pages, for personal records and lawful evidence preservation.

## 1. Purpose

AutoScreenShot was created to help users preserve long records that are difficult to capture manually. For example, users may need to keep complete chat histories, transaction records, or other screen content for personal records, dispute handling, or lawful evidence preservation.

## 2. Privacy and Transparency

AutoScreenShot is designed to run its core capture workflow on your device.

- **Local Processing:** The app performs screen capture and scrolling automation locally on your device.
- **User-Controlled Operation:** Screen capture and automated scrolling only start after you explicitly grant the required Android system permissions and start a capture task.
- **No Internet Requirement:** The app does not require an internet connection for its core screenshot and scrolling functions.

## 3. Data Handling

- **No Data Collection by the Developer:** We do not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal information, screenshots, screen content, messages, or metadata.
- **On-Device Storage:** Generated screenshots are saved directly on your device in the system media storage, under the `DCIM/AutoScreenShot` folder.
- **No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising SDKs:** The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs that send your evidence or usage data to third parties.
- **No Account System:** The app does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, phone number, or other identity information to use the core features.

## 4. Permissions Disclosure

AutoScreenShot uses the following Android permissions and system capabilities to provide its capture workflow.

### MediaProjection / Screen Capture

- **Use:** Captures the visible content of your screen to create screenshot images.
- **User Control:** Android displays a system-level screen capture permission dialog before capture starts. Capture only begins after you approve this prompt.
- **Data Handling:** Captured images are saved locally on your device and are not uploaded by the app.

### Accessibility Service

- **Use:** Identifies scrollable areas on the active screen and performs scroll gestures. This allows the app to automate captures of long content.
- **Safety:** We do **not** use the Accessibility Service to monitor your typing, collect text, record keystrokes, read private messages, or send accessibility data to the developer or any third party.
- **Scope:** The service is used only for the user-requested capture workflow and related scrolling automation.

### Display Over Other Apps

- **Use:** Shows a small floating control panel while you are capturing content in another app.
- **Safety:** The overlay is used for capture controls only. It is not used to collect information from other apps.

### Foreground Service and Notifications

- **Use:** Runs an active capture session as a foreground service so Android can keep the task running and show an ongoing notification.
- **Safety:** The service is tied to the active capture workflow and stops when the task ends or the user stops it.

### Storage / Media Store

- **Use:** Saves screenshots to your device's media storage using Android's scoped storage system.
- **Your Control:** You can view, move, share, or delete the generated screenshots using your device's gallery or file manager.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

AutoScreenShot does not store your screenshots on developer-controlled servers. Screenshots remain on your device until you delete them. To delete captured screenshots, remove them from your gallery or file manager in the `DCIM/AutoScreenShot` folder.

## 6. Data Security

Because AutoScreenShot does not upload screenshots or personal data to developer-controlled servers, security mainly depends on your device security settings, Android permissions, and any apps or cloud backup services you choose to use outside AutoScreenShot.

## 7. Children

AutoScreenShot is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Ethical Use

This tool is intended for legitimate personal record keeping and lawful evidence preservation. You are responsible for making sure that any content you capture is content you are legally allowed to access and preserve. The developer prohibits using this app for illegal surveillance, harassment, stalking, unauthorized monitoring, capturing another person's private information without permission, or other privacy-invasive activities.
